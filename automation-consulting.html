Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our general digital services

How quickly can these digital solutions be implemented?

+

Most of our general digital services can be implemented within 1-2 weeks, with simpler solutions like QR codes and Google Business Profile setup often completed within days. The exact timeline depends on the complexity of your requirements and the level of customization needed. We prioritize rapid deployment to ensure you start seeing benefits as quickly as possible.

Do I need technical knowledge to manage these tools after implementation?

+

No, our solutions are designed to be user-friendly and manageable without technical expertise. We provide comprehensive training and documentation, and our ongoing support ensures you're never left struggling with technical issues. For more complex systems, we offer management services if you prefer not to handle them in-house.

Can these solutions integrate with my existing systems?

+

Yes, our digital solutions are designed to integrate seamlessly with most common business systems and platforms. During the discovery phase, we'll assess your current technology stack and ensure compatibility. Our automation solutions in particular excel at connecting different systems and creating efficient workflows between them.

What kind of ongoing costs should I expect?

+

Ongoing costs vary by solution. Some services like QR codes have minimal or no recurring costs once created. Others like booking systems or automation platforms typically involve monthly subscription fees to the underlying platforms. We're transparent about all costs upfront and focus on solutions that deliver clear ROI to ensure the value far exceeds the investment.

How do I know which digital solutions are right for my business?

+

We begin every engagement with a thorough discovery process to understand your business goals, challenges, and current operations. Based on this assessment, we recommend the solutions that will deliver the most value for your specific situation. We're focused on practical results, not selling you services you don't need.

Do you offer packages or bundles of these services?

+

Yes, we offer several service bundles designed for specific business types and needs. For example, our "Restaurant Digital Package" includes online menu, QR codes, Google Business Profile, and booking system. These packages offer cost savings compared to implementing each service separately. We can also create custom bundles based on your specific requirements.
