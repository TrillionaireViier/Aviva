EcoGreen Complete Rebrand: Award-Winning Design

Creating a sustainable brand identity that won the Green Design Award 2024

Client EcoGreen Solutions
Industry Environmental Technology
Duration 5 Months
Services Brand Identity & Design

The Challenge

EcoGreen Solutions was an innovative environmental technology company with groundbreaking products but a dated brand identity that didn't reflect their cutting-edge approach to sustainability. They needed a complete rebrand that would position them as industry leaders while communicating their environmental values effectively.

Our Design Approach

We developed a comprehensive brand identity that seamlessly blended modern design principles with environmental consciousness. Our approach focused on creating a visual language that would resonate with both B2B clients and environmentally conscious consumers.

  • Logo design inspired by natural growth patterns
  • Sustainable color palette using earth tones and vibrant greens
  • Typography system balancing professionalism with approachability
  • Comprehensive brand guidelines and asset library
  • Application across digital and print materials

Design Elements That Made the Difference

The success of the rebrand was built on several key design decisions that perfectly captured EcoGreen's mission and values:

  • Organic Logo Design: Created using golden ratio principles inspired by leaf growth patterns
  • Sustainable Color System: Carefully selected colors that evoke nature while maintaining professional appeal
  • Flexible Brand System: Designed to work across all touchpoints from business cards to billboards
  • Environmental Messaging: Integrated sustainability messaging into every brand element
