PropFinder Real Estate Platform

Revolutionizing Property Search with AI and Virtual Tours

Web Development 2024 8 Months

The Challenge

PropFinder, a growing real estate agency, was struggling with outdated property listing methods and losing potential buyers to competitors with better online experiences. They needed a modern platform that could showcase properties effectively and streamline the buying process.

Our Solution

We developed a comprehensive real estate platform featuring:

  • AI-Powered Property Matching: Smart algorithms that match buyers with ideal properties based on preferences and behavior
  • Virtual Tour Integration: 360° property tours allowing remote viewing experiences
  • Advanced Search Filters: Intuitive filtering system with map integration and neighborhood insights
  • Mobile-First Design: Responsive platform optimized for property browsing on all devices
  • Agent Dashboard: Comprehensive CRM for managing leads, properties, and client communications
