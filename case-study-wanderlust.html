WanderLust Travel Branding

Inspiring Adventure Through Compelling Brand Identity

Brand Design 2024 6 Months

The Challenge

WanderLust Travel was a traditional travel agency struggling to connect with younger, adventure-seeking travelers. They needed a complete brand transformation that would position them as the go-to agency for unique, experiential travel while maintaining trust and professionalism.

Our Design Solution

We created a comprehensive brand identity system that captures the spirit of adventure:

  • Adventure-Focused Logo: Dynamic compass-inspired design symbolizing exploration and discovery
  • Vibrant Color Palette: Earth tones and adventure colors that evoke wanderlust and natural beauty
  • Typography System: Modern, readable fonts that work across digital and print materials
  • Photography Style: Authentic, experiential travel photography showcasing real adventures
  • Brand Guidelines: Comprehensive style guide ensuring consistent brand application
