Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services and process

What services does Aviva Agency offer?

+

We offer comprehensive digital marketing services including social media marketing, web development, SEO optimization, brand design, mobile app development, and business automation solutions. Our team specializes in creating custom strategies for each client's unique needs.

How long does a typical project take?

+

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. Social media campaigns typically launch within 1-2 weeks, while custom web development projects range from 4-12 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation and keep you updated throughout the process.

Do you work with small businesses or only large corporations?

+

We work with businesses of all sizes, from startups and small local businesses to large corporations. Our scalable solutions and flexible pricing ensure that every business can benefit from professional digital marketing and development services.

What makes Aviva Agency different from other digital agencies?

+

Our unique combination of creative expertise and technical proficiency sets us apart. We focus on measurable results, provide transparent reporting, offer 24/7 support, and maintain long-term partnerships with our clients. Our 7-step proven process ensures consistent quality and client satisfaction.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

+

Yes, we offer comprehensive post-launch support including maintenance, updates, performance monitoring, and optimization. We believe in long-term partnerships and provide various support packages to ensure your digital presence continues to grow and succeed.

How do you measure the success of your campaigns?

+

We use comprehensive analytics and KPI tracking to measure success. This includes website traffic, conversion rates, social media engagement, lead generation, ROI, and other metrics specific to your goals. We provide regular reports and insights to keep you informed of your campaign's performance.

What is your pricing structure?

+

Our pricing is project-based and depends on scope, complexity, and timeline. We offer competitive rates with transparent pricing - no hidden fees. During our consultation, we'll provide a detailed quote tailored to your specific needs and budget. We also offer flexible payment plans for larger projects.
